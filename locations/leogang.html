Leogang is synonymous with World Cup downhill racing. The Bikepark Leogang hosts a round of the UCI DH World Cup almost every year, and the Speedster track is one of the most iconic courses on the circuit. High-speed, physically demanding, with massive jumps and technical rock gardens.

The bike park also caters to all levels with excellent flow trails and a skills area. But make no mistake -- the pro-line features are where Leogang earns its reputation. The infamous motorway section is a flat-out sprint that separates the fast from the fearless, demanding perfect bike setup and nerve.

Beyond the race track, the Saalfelden-Leogang region offers stunning Austrian alpine scenery, well-maintained trails through dense forest, and a bike park infrastructure that rivals anywhere in Europe. Whether you're sessioning the Speedster or cruising the flow lines, every run ends with a view of the Leoganger Steinberge that makes the uplift queue worth the wait.

"The Speedster finish line has seen more drama than any other track in World Cup history."

TRAIL HIGHLIGHTS

Speedster

Expert

3.4km • 550m vertical drop

The legendary World Cup course. A relentless combination of high-speed straights, massive gap jumps, and punishing rock gardens. The motorway section demands full commitment at race speed.

Hot Shot

Advanced

2.8km • 450m vertical drop

A fast, technical trail with natural features and well-built jumps. Bridges the gap between flow trails and the full World Cup experience. Perfect for building confidence at speed.

Hangman I

Intermediate

2.1km • 350m vertical drop

A well-shaped flow trail with smooth berms, gentle jumps, and forgiving lines. The ideal warm-up trail and a great introduction to Leogang's terrain before stepping up to the bigger lines.

Steinberg Trail

Intermediate

4.2km • 400m vertical drop

A longer trail winding through forest with views of the Steinberge massif. Rolling terrain, natural features, and enough variety to keep intermediate riders engaged for the full descent.

GETTING THERE

Nearest Airport

Salzburg Airport (SZG) -- 1.5 hour drive. Munich Airport (MUC) -- 2.5 hour drive.

By Car

A10 motorway from Salzburg, exit at Leogang. Well-signposted. Free parking at the bike park base.

Public Transport

Train to Leogang station via Salzburg. Local bus connects the station to the bike park during summer season.

PRO TIPS

1

Walk the Speedster First

Before dropping in, walk the course to inspect the gaps and rock gardens. Knowing what's coming at speed is the difference between a clean run and a hospital visit.

2

Ride Hangman for Warm-Up

Hangman I is the perfect warm-up trail. Get your legs and eyes dialed in on the flow lines before committing to the steeper, more technical tracks.

3

Visit the Bike Wash Station

Leogang's bike wash facilities are excellent and free to use. Clean your bike after muddy runs to keep your suspension and drivetrain performing at their best.

4

Stay for the World Cup

If your timing aligns with the UCI World Cup round, stay for the atmosphere. Watching the pros send it down the Speedster in person is an unforgettable experience.
